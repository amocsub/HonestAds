HonestAds

HonestAds is a web service that lets you search through advertisers easily and share queries with others. Create filters, copy the URL, and monitor who might be publishing Google Ads on your domain with more control than the official Transparency Center.

Made with love by Mati Busco. Contributions are welcome at github.com/amocsub/HonestAds. If you’d like to support the project you can buy me a beer.
